    How it works

    This page gives the overview of ReportACrime.ng

    File A Report

    Feel free to file a report if you are a victim of a crime-case and let neccessary bodies fight your cause for you. Also the Criminal after found guilty will be displayed on our web page for other Nigerians to beware.

    Criminal Database

    The Criminal Database is the repository for criminals Information after found guilty by the law. This database will aide background check for any individual you want to deal with perhaps on business, marriage, employment e.t.c, so you won't fall a victim.

    Confidentiality

    Your personal information(the reporter) is kept private if you plead anonymous.
